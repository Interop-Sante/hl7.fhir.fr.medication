Data Type Profile: Range with UCUM or EDQM quantity units

Official URL: https://hl7.fr/ig/fhir/medication/StructureDefinition/FrRangeMedication Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2025-07-21 Computable Name: FrRangeMedication

Range with low and high unit UCUM or EDQM encoded

Usages:

  • Use this DataType Profile: FrCurrentMedicationMedicationStatement, FrInpatientMedicationRequest, FrMedicationHistoryMedicationStatement, FrMedicationReconciliationMedicationStatement and FrMedicationRequest
