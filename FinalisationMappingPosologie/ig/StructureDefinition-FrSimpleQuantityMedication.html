Data Type Profile: SimpleQuantity with UCUM or EDQM quantity unit

Official URL: https://hl7.fr/fhir/fr/medication/StructureDefinition/FrSimpleQuantityMedication Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2025-04-14 Computable Name: FrSimpleQuantityMedication

simple quantity datatype requiring a UCUM or EDQM unit

Usage:

  • Use this DataType Constraint: Range with UCUM or EDQM quantity units, Ratio with UCUM or EDQM quantity units, FrCurrentMedicationMedicationStatement, FrInpatientMedicationRequest... Show 2 more, FrMedicationHistoryMedicationStatement and FrMedicationReconciliationMedicationStatement
